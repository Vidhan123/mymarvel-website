Iron Man

After being kidnapped by a powerful terrorist organization, brilliant industrialist Tony Stark narrowly escapes using an iron suit he crafted from scrap metal and spare parts. Upon returning home, he devotes all of his time and resources to perfecting the iron man suit, and using it to fight evil, sometimes in the place where he least expects it.

Source: Movieclips Classic Trailers, IMDb

Iron Man 2

With the world now aware of his dual life as the armored superhero Iron Man, billionaire inventor Tony Stark faces pressure from the government to share his technology with the military. Unwilling to let go of his invention, Stark, along with Pepper, and James at his side, must forge new alliances - and confront powerful enemies.

Source: Movieclips, IMDb

Iron Man 3

After saving New York from an alien invasion, billionaire Tony Stark buries himself deeper into his work by building a ton of new and improved Iron Man suits. When his home is demolished and his suits are destroyed, Stark begins a personal journey to finding out the truth about the bombings and tracking down whoever is truly responsible.

Iron Man is dead???

It is rather ironic for the Invincible Iron Man to turn out not to be invincible, no?

All those who wept at the end of Avengers: Endgame when Ironman snaps away Thanos and his army of weird creatures and along with it embraces his own death — this one is for you.

None of us who watched the Endgame would have quite expected the Invincible Iron Man to actually die. After all, he joined the time heist almost at the fag end (although he was the one who fixed it right) and had the most to lose. You would think, for the cool and self-assured Tony Stark it was just another regular day at work before returning home to his little girl. But the sad truth is that he didn’t.

For the philosophical fans it is the end of an era but for some never-say-die ones like me there is another way to look at it; through the lens of Quantum Mechanics.Through this loophole, Iron Man technically does not have to have died (at least not in the universe we see on screen). So, for all those Iron Man fans out there who love him 3,000: stop looking so glum.

He’s called the Invincible Iron Man for a reason.
